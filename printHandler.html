Some recommended settings

  • scale: 100%
  • Paper size: A3
  • Margins: None
  • Margins: None
  • Print Backgrounds: True (You'll probably see nothing without this)

Play around with the settings though, you want each page to fit on its own page (you can easily trim borders with other programs)
